VCAL-SPHERE: Hybrid pipeline for reduction of VLT/SPHERE data

Christiaens et al. (including Balsalobre-Ruza) 2023, ASCL

VCAL-SPHERE is a flexible pipeline for VLT/SPHERE high-contrast imaging, handling calibration, pre- and post-processing for IFS and IRDIS data. It combines EsoRex workflows with VIP routines for optimal reduction of exoplanet and disk observations.

KOBEsim: A Bayesian observing strategy algorithm for planet detection in radial velocity blind-search surveys

Balsalobre-Ruza et al. 2022, A&A, 669, A18

The KOBEsim algorithm schedules radial velocity measurements to maximize the Bayesian evidence for emerging periodic signals. Tests on simulations and real data show it can halve the time and number of observations needed to confirm low-mass exoplanets.

The KOBE experiment: K-dwarfs Orbited By habitable Exoplanets. Project goals, target selection, and stellar characterization

Lillo-Box et al. (including Balsalobre-Ruza) 2022, A&A, 667, A102

The KOBE survey is the first dedicated radial velocity search for habitable-zone planets around late K-dwarfs, ideal hosts due to their low activity and accessible habitable zones. Monitoring 50 stars with CARMENES, KOBE expects to find ~1.7 planets per star.
